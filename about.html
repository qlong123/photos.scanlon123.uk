About Us

Welcome to Scanlon123 Photography, where we specialize in professional photography for children's parties. Our mission is to capture memories and promote joyful moments with high-quality images that truly reflect the fun and excitement of your celebrations.

We believe in providing fair prices without compromising on quality. With a focus on excellent technique and a commitment to fast turnaround times (just 3 days!), you can trust that your photos will be ready when you need them.

We also offer the option to have your photos professionally printed and delivered right to your door for an additional cost of around £30 per print.

Our goal is to create an experience that leaves clients feeling happy and satisfied with the memories we capture.

Let’s make your next children's party unforgettable together!
